# Option 5: Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku

## Shape

11 days / 10 nights.

Number of travel days: 5

- Day 1: arrive in Tokyo
- Day 4: Tokyo -> Setouchi base
- Day 7: Setouchi base -> Onomichi / Imabari side
- Day 9: Onomichi / Imabari side -> Matsuyama
- Day 11: depart via western Japan

- Tokyo: 3 nights
- Setouchi / art-island base: 3 nights
- Onomichi or Imabari side: 2 nights
- Matsuyama: 2 nights

Suggested flights:

- SFO to Tokyo nonstop
- depart via western Japan rather than returning all the way to Tokyo

Transit overview:

- SFO -> Tokyo keeps the start simple
- Tokyo -> western Japan is a longer shinkansen move but still cleaner than adding a domestic flight
- ferries and short rail moves are part of the route identity here and need to be planned in advance
- Onomichi / Imabari -> Matsuyama is manageable but adds one more overland move
- departure works best from western Japan rather than returning to Tokyo

## Best timing

June works well.

## Section highlights

### Tokyo

Overview:

- landing zone
- city autonomy
- easiest food and shopping section

Activities:

- [Tokyo destination overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kanto/tokyo/)
- [Kiyosumi Garden](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1700/)
- [Shimokitazawa](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2108/)
- Daikanyama / Nakameguro wandering
- one strong dinner night

### Setouchi islands / art side

Overview:

- calmest middle section
- art-and-islands block
- best place for a polished splurge stay

Activities:

- [Setouchi Islands overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/shikoku/kagawa/setouchi-islands/)
- [Naoshima](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/220/)
- [Teshima](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/835/)
- Shodoshima / Angel Road day
- ferry day
- design / museum stops
- seaside cafes
- beach-view lunch

### Onomichi / Imabari + Matsuyama

Overview:

- active core plus softer finish
- best movement section
- strongest lower-city-share identity

Activities:

- [Shimanami Kaido](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/822/)
- [Cycling the Shimanami Kaido itinerary](https://www.japan.travel/en/itineraries/cycling-the-shimanami-kaido/)
- Onomichi wandering
- shorter scenic ride
- coast viewpoints
- [Matsuyama overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/shikoku/ehime/matsuyama-and-around/)
- [Dogo Onsen](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/821/)
- castle-town evening

## Candidate itinerary

### Day 1: Arrive in Tokyo

- check in
- easy neighborhood walk
- early dinner

### Day 2: Tokyo shared city day

- coffee-and-walk morning
- suggested area: Daikanyama / Nakameguro, or Kiyosumi-Shirakawa if coffee is the priority
- shared lunch
- split afternoon
  - Track A: shopping / vintage / cafes
  - Track B: gym / run / photo walk
  - Track C: free city wandering
- one strong dinner

### Day 3: Tokyo flexible day

- morning free
- optional thrift / shopping branch in [Shimokitazawa](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2108/)
- optional gym / run / photo walk branch
- easy dinner

### Day 4: Transfer west

- long rail move
- check in
- easy evening

### Day 5: Shared Setouchi island / art day

- ferry movement
- [Naoshima](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/220/) or [Teshima](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/835/) art / design block
- slower lunch and views
- low-coordination evening

### Day 6: Split Setouchi day

- Track A: island wandering / cafes / museums
- Track B: ferry-hopping / active day
- Track C: photography / slower scenic day

### Day 7: Transfer to the Shimanami base

- move to Onomichi or Imabari side
- easy evening

### Day 8: Active core day

- full or partial [Shimanami Kaido](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/822/)
- shorter scenic version for less athletic travelers
- non-bike scenic alternative if needed

### Day 9: Move to Matsuyama

- transfer day
- onsen / town evening

### Day 10: Matsuyama finish day

- castle-town wandering
- [Dogo Onsen](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/821/) time
- final dinner

### Day 11: Departure via western Japan

- keep this day clean
- do not add a final sightseeing block
