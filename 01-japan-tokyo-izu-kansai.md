# Option 1: Tokyo + Izu + Kansai

## Shape

11 days / 10 nights.

Number of travel days: 4

- Day 1: arrive in Tokyo
- Day 4: Tokyo -> Shimoda
- Day 9: Shimoda -> Osaka
- Day 11: depart from Osaka

- Tokyo: 3 nights
- Shimoda / South Izu: 5 nights
- Osaka: 2 nights

Suggested flights:

- SFO to Tokyo nonstop
- Osaka to SFO nonstop if schedules and pricing cooperate

Transit overview:

- SFO -> Tokyo is the cleanest long-haul start
- Tokyo -> Shimoda is one longer rail transfer and should be treated as an early-start travel day
- Shimoda -> Osaka is the longest surface move in the trip and works best as a dedicated transfer day
- Osaka is the simplest departure base in this version

## Best timing

Any June window can work.

Late June is slightly better if warmer beach weather matters more.

- city autonomy

- one strong dinner night

### Shimoda / South Izu

Overview:

- trip highlight
- beach-and-coast section
- best place for the splurge stay

Activities:

- [Izu Peninsula overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/tokai/shizuoka/izu-and-around/)

- boat outing
- snorkeling day

### Osaka

Overview:

- optional run / gym / sauna block

## Candidate itinerary

### Day 1: Arrive in Tokyo

- check in
- easy neighborhood walk
- early casual dinner

### Day 2: Tokyo shared city day

- coffee-and-walk morning
- suggested area: Daikanyama / Nakameguro, or Kiyosumi-Shirakawa if coffee is the priority
- shared lunch
- afternoon split
  - Track A: shopping / vintage / cafe crawl
  - Track B: gym / run / photo walk
  - Track C: free city wandering
- one strong dinner

### Day 3: Tokyo flexible day

- morning free
- optional [Ueno Zoological Gardens](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1674/) or [Tokyo Sea Life Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1637/) branch
- optional thrift / shopping branch in [Shimokitazawa](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2108/)
- optional gym / run / photo walk branch
- easy or splurge dinner

### Day 4: Early transfer to Shimoda

- early start from Tokyo
- arrive in [Shimoda](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2036/)
- check in and decompress
- easy walk on the beach or through the old town
- relaxed dinner

### Day 5: Shared South Izu nature day

- one moderate scenic coast route, ideally a single cape / cliff / cove route rather than multiple scattered stops
- late lunch in town or by the coast
- easy beach time at the end of the day
- low-key evening

### Day 6: Split beach and water day

- Track A: beach morning at [Shirahama Beach](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1273/) or Tatadohama, relaxed lunch, hotel downtime
- Track B: surfing, boat outing, snorkeling, or another more active coastal session
- Track C: aquarium, cafes, photography, and a slower coastal stop
- regroup for dinner

### Day 7: Premium Shimoda day

- slow breakfast with a view
- half-day free with predefined options
  - easier beach / pool day
  - Perry Road / old-town wandering and cafes
  - scenic drive or lookout if you use a car locally
  - second active morning for P&L and the more active half
- very good dinner

### Day 8: South Izu flex day

- weather and energy buffer
- repeat the best activity from earlier in the stay
  - second surf or boat outing
  - longer beach day
  - aquarium or another animal-focused branch
  - moderate scenic outing to a cape or lookout

### Day 9: Transfer to Osaka

- transfer from Shimoda to Osaka
- check in
- easy dinner in one lively neighborhood

### Day 10: Osaka final flex day

- coffee and wandering in the morning
- suggested area: [Dotonbori & Shinsaibashi](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kansai/osaka/dotonbori-and-shinsaibashi/) or [Namba](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kansai/osaka/nanba/)
- shared lunch
- optional split in the afternoon
  - Branch 1: Juan / Steph / Christian / Eliza go to [Nara Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/ma_150/)
  - Branch 2: Phil / Lillian keep it active with a self-directed outing, run, gym, or another Kansai-side excursion
- fun final dinner neighborhood at night

### Day 11: Final Osaka day and departure

- relaxed breakfast
- short final shopping or coffee window
- depart from Osaka
