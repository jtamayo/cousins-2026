# Option 2: Tokyo + Okinawa

## Shape

11 days / 10 nights.

Number of travel days: 4

- Day 1: arrive in Tokyo
- Day 4: Tokyo -> Okinawa
- Day 9: Okinawa -> Osaka
- Day 11: depart from Osaka

- Tokyo: 3 nights
- Okinawa Main Island: 5 nights
- Osaka: 2 nights

Suggested flights:

- SFO to Tokyo nonstop
- Tokyo to Okinawa domestic flight
- Okinawa to Osaka domestic flight
- Osaka to SFO nonstop if practical

Transit overview:

- SFO -> Tokyo keeps the start simple
- Tokyo -> Okinawa is the first domestic flight and starts the main beach section
- Okinawa -> Osaka is the second domestic flight and is the main added friction in this option
- Osaka works best as a short finishing base and departure city

## Best timing

Late June only.

## Section highlights

### Tokyo

Overview:

- landing zone
- city autonomy
- easiest food and shopping section

Activities:

- [Tokyo destination overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kanto/tokyo/)
- [Ueno Zoological Gardens](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1674/)
- [Tokyo Sea Life Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1637/)
- [Kiyosumi Garden](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1700/)
- [Shimokitazawa](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2108/)
- Daikanyama / Nakameguro wandering
- one strong dinner night

### Okinawa Main Island

Overview:

- tropical core of the trip
- resort-and-water section
- best place for the splurge stay

Activities:

- [Okinawa Main Island overview](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/okinawa/okinawa/)
- [Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/581/)
- beach day
- pool day
- surf lesson
- snorkel / dive outing
- boat day
- scenic coast drive to northern viewpoints
- cafe / sunset day

### Osaka

Overview:

- short finishing base
- simple rebound section
- departure city

Activities:

- [Namba](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kansai/osaka/nanba/)
- [Dotonbori & Shinsaibashi](https://www.japan.travel/en/destinations/kansai/osaka/dotonbori-and-shinsaibashi/)
- final shopping block
- one high-energy dinner night
- optional [Nara Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/ma_150/) branch
- low-stakes city wandering

## Candidate itinerary

### Day 1: Arrive in Tokyo

- check in
- easy neighborhood walk
- early dinner

### Day 2: Tokyo shared city day

- coffee-and-walk morning
- suggested area: Daikanyama / Nakameguro, or Kiyosumi-Shirakawa if coffee is the priority
- shared lunch
- split afternoon
  - Track A: shopping / vintage / cafes
  - Track B: gym / run / photo walk
  - Track C: free city wandering
- one strong dinner

### Day 3: Tokyo flexible day

- morning free
- optional [Ueno Zoological Gardens](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1674/) or [Tokyo Sea Life Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/1637/) branch
- optional thrift / shopping branch in [Shimokitazawa](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/2108/)
- optional gym / run / photo walk branch
- easy or splurge dinner

### Day 4: Fly to Okinawa

- morning or midday flight
- check in
- easy coast evening
- relaxed dinner

### Day 5: Shared beach day

- one clear beach plan
- long lunch
- easy pool / coast afternoon
- sunset drinks or dinner

### Day 6: Split water day

- Track A: beach / pool / spa
- Track B: surf / snorkel / dive / boat
- Track C: [Okinawa Churaumi Aquarium](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/581/) / cafe / scenic wandering
- regroup for dinner

### Day 7: Premium Okinawa day

- slow breakfast
- scenic drive or coast wandering
- easy premium afternoon
- strong dinner

### Day 8: Weather buffer day

- repeat the best beach or water activity from earlier in the stay
- keep bookings flexible until closer to the trip

### Day 9: Fly to Osaka

- domestic flight to Osaka
- check in
- easy dinner

### Day 10: Osaka final flex day

- food / shopping / coffee
- optional [Nara Park](https://www.japan.travel/en/spot/ma_150/) or city-only split
- final dinner

### Day 11: Departure from Osaka

- relaxed breakfast
- depart
