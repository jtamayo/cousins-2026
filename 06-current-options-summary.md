# Japan Options — Quick Summary

Read this first. Each option has a detailed file linked below.

## The five options

### 1. Tokyo + Izu + Osaka → [full itinerary](01-japan-tokyo-izu-kansai.md)
**Easiest group trip.** Beaches, onsen, and seafood on the Izu coast, bookended by Tokyo and Osaka. No domestic flights.
- Watch-out: June rain undercuts beach plans. Five nights in Shimoda may be too long in bad weather.

### 2. Tokyo + Okinawa + Osaka → [full itinerary](02-japan-tokyo-okinawa.md)
**Best beach fantasy.** The only option with warm tropical water and resort pool days. Late June only.
- Watch-out: Highest weather risk. Two domestic flights. Main island beaches underwhelm — need Kerama Islands side trip for the real deal.

### 3. Tokyo + Hokkaido → [full itinerary](03-japan-tokyo-hokkaido.md)
**Safest June weather.** Hokkaido skips rainy season entirely. Sapporo food, volcanic lakes, hiking, onsen, 15+ hours of daylight.
- Watch-out: No warm water or beach. Rental car needed. Departure connects through Tokyo.

### 4. Tokyo + Hokuriku + Alps → [full itinerary](04-japan-tokyo-hokuriku-alps.md)
**Best mountain-and-town route.** Kanazawa seafood, Takayama old town, Kamikochi alpine valley. Most unusual itinerary, best food overall.
- Watch-out: Most coordination and least split-day freedom. Bus seats for six need advance booking. No Kyoto/Osaka.

### 5. Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku → [full itinerary](05-japan-tokyo-setouchi-shikoku.md)
**Most varied day-to-day.** World-class art islands, a legendary cycling route, Japan's oldest hot spring. No two days alike.
- Watch-out: Most advance planning (timed art tickets, e-bike reservations, Monday closures). Rain can disrupt cycling.

## Current ranking

1. **Izu + Kansai** — if the trip should be easiest to say yes to
2. **Setouchi + Shikoku** — if the trip should be the most rewarding
3. **Hokkaido** — if June weather confidence matters most
4. **Hokuriku + Alps** — if the group wants something truly different
5. **Okinawa** — if warm beach water is non-negotiable and you can go late June

## How to decide

Pick based on what your group values most:

| Priority | Best option |
|---|---|
| Easiest consensus | Izu + Kansai |
| Best weather | Hokkaido |
| Most variety | Setouchi + Shikoku |
| Best food | Hokuriku + Alps (closely followed by Hokkaido) |
| Warm water / beach | Okinawa (only option) |
| Most active / outdoors | Hokkaido or Setouchi + Shikoku |
| Least planning required | Izu + Kansai |
| Most planning required | Setouchi + Shikoku |
