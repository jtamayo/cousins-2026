# Trip Requirements Summary

## Core Trip Requirements

- Group size: 6 people, traveling as 3 couples
- Timing: must be in June
- Trip length: roughly 10 to 12 shared days, though each couple might extend it before or after
- Origin: flying from San Francisco

- Eliza likes:

  - some drinks and nightlife
  - luxury accommodations
  - animals, she visits zoos and wants to go to galapagos
- Eliza does not want overly strenuous hiking, but moderate outdoor activities are fine
- Eliza is allergic to salmon and molluscs

## What the Group Needs Overall

- A trip that balances activity, comfort, some form of night life, and nature
- A trip that has something special for everyone, since everyone has generally traveled quite a bit
- A trip where there's room for everyone to have great times together, but for everyone to also have memorable experiences alone or in couples
- A route that does not depend on constant real-time planning or "we'll figure it out when we get there"; this group is really really bad at making last minute decisions


## Likely Hard Constraints

- Must work in June. We've agreed on June 9-25 as they days when we'll be travelling though haven't bought tickets yet
- Needs good enough vegetarian/food flexibility for Stephanie and Eliza
- Should include at least some real outdoor and adventure value
- Must have backup alternatives for pregnant women that cannot use a hot tub or go scuba diving

## Likely Soft Constraints

- Some animal activities are important, but not everyone needs to join
- Shopping matters, but not enough to justify making the whole trip city-heavy
- Too many hotel changes could be an issue
- Should include one splurge stay, preferably where prices are favorable
