# Trip Requirements Summary

## Core Trip Requirements

- Group size: 6 people, traveling as 3 couples
- Timing: must be in June
- Trip length: roughly 10 days, perhaps 12 if worth it
- Origin: flying from San Francisco


## Couple-by-Couple Preferences

### Christian & Eliza

- Want warm weather
- Christian likes:
  - hiking
  - beach time
  - sauna and athletic activities
  - beautiful landscapes and instagram worthy photos
- Eliza likes:
  - shopping
  - good dinners
  - some drinks and nightlife
  - luxury accommodations
  - animals, she visits zoos and wants to go to galapagos
- Eliza does not want strenuous hiking, but lighter outdoor activities are fine

### Phil & Lillian

- Most outdoor and adventure-oriented couple
- Like:
  - long hikes
  - bike rides
  - surfing
  - snorkeling
  - scuba diving
  - generally more intense active days and adrenaline
- They do not drink alcohol
- They like Tokyo, but too much city time could make the trip feel less worth it
- Important health consideration:
  - Lillian may be early in pregnancy by June, so some activities may need to remain optional
- Best fit overall: a trip with a strong active identity, not just cities plus a few token outdoor days

### Juan & Steph

- Both like being active
- Both have large tattoos that are difficult to cover in a bathing suit or when naked
- Both enjoy:
  - beautiful nature
  - cities
  - exploring towns
- Steph likes:
  - thrift shopping
  - animal viewing and interaction: she's loved safaris
  - vegetarian food access or sushi is important
- Juan likes:
  - coffee
  - people-watching
  - some shopping, particularly for tools and kitchen gadgets

## What the Group Needs Overall

- A trip that balances activity, comfort, cities, and nature
- A trip that has something special for everyone, since everyone has already been to Japan and has generally traveled quite a bit
- A trip where there's room for everyone to have great times together, but for everyone to also have memorable experiences alone or in couples
- A route that does not depend on constant real-time planning or "we'll figure it out when we get there"; this group is really really bad at making last minute decisions


## Likely Hard Constraints

- Must work in June, though the exact dates are flexible
- Needs good enough vegetarian/sushi flexibility
- Should include at least some real outdoor and adventure value
- Must have backup alternatives for pregnant women that cannot use a hot tub or go scuba diving

## Likely Soft Constraints

- Some animal activities are important, but not everyone needs to join
- Shopping matters, but not enough to justify making the whole trip city-heavy
- The trip should feel exciting enough to compete with alternatives like Azores, Madeira, Sicily, Greece, or Turkey
- Too many hotel changes could be an issue
- Should include one splurge stay, preferably where prices are favorable
