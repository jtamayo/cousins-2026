## Notes

- Prices are for standard/midsize cars (assumed). May need to confirm size for 3 people + luggage per car
- June is peak season — book early
- Norway tolls are electronic (AutoPASS) and usually billed to the rental company and charged to your card after the trip
- Gas is expensive in Norway (~$8–9/gallon); consider if any rentals offer electric/hybrid options
