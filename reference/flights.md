## C&E — Option B southbound / Option C (into Alesund, home from Bergen)

### SFO → Alesund (Jun 9)

| Leg | Depart | Arrive | Duration | Airline | Aircraft | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SFO → AMS | 1:45 PM Jun 9 | 9:05 AM Jun 10 | 10h 20m | KLM KL 606 | 787-10 | Overnight, 31" legroom, Wi-Fi ($), USB |
| AMS → AES | 2:20 PM Jun 10 | 4:20 PM Jun 10 | 2h | KLM KL 1157 | E175 | Operated by KLM Cityhopper, 29" legroom |

- Layover in AMS: 5h 15m (long — but Amsterdam Schiphol is comfortable)
- CO2: 561 kg (20% below average)

### Bergen → SFO (Jun 24)

| Leg | Depart | Arrive | Duration | Airline | Aircraft | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| BGO → AMS | 6:00 AM Jun 24 | 7:45 AM | 1h 45m | KLM KL 1162 | 737 | Very early departure |
| AMS → SFO | 9:50 AM | 11:45 AM | 10h 55m | KLM KL 605 | 787-10 | 31" legroom, Wi-Fi ($), USB |

- Layover in AMS: 2h 5m
- CO2: 470 kg (29% below average)

### Summary

- Arrives Alesund: 4:20 PM Jun 10 (long travel day — 5h layover in AMS)
- Returns from Bergen: 6:00 AM Jun 24 (very early — need ~3 AM airport arrival)
- Jun 19–24: 5 nights in/around Bergen, or Bergen + a side trip
- All flights on KLM — bookable as multi-city

### Implications

- **Favors southbound direction** (Alesund → Bergen) or **Option C** (Stavanger → Bergen): C&E end in Bergen and fly home from there
- The 5h15m AMS layover on the way in is noticeably worse than the 2h10m CPH layover in the northbound/SAS option
- The 6:00 AM departure home is rough
- **Copenhagen gap**: if C&E want Copenhagen, they'd need to fly BGO → CPH and then back to BGO for the flight home (awkward), or skip Copenhagen and spend the post-split days in Bergen or elsewhere reachable by land
- **vs. the northbound SAS option**: the northbound itinerary gives C&E a cleaner path to Copenhagen (AES → CPH direct, then CPH → SFO direct) with shorter layovers and no 6 AM flight
