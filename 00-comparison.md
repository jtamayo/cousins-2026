# Japan Options Comparison

## Decision frame

There are five live options.

The choice is mostly between two trip identities:

- `Tokyo + Izu + Kansai`: easiest consensus, easiest food and shopping, easiest split-day freedom
- `Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku`: lower city share, more movement, more distinctive repeat-Japan feel

The other three options are more specialized:

- `Tokyo + Hokkaido`: best June weather hedge
- `Tokyo + Hokuriku + Alps`: best mountain-and-town route
- `Tokyo + Okinawa`: highest beach upside, highest weather risk

## Option snapshot

### Tokyo + Izu + Kansai

File: `01-japan-tokyo-izu-kansai.md`

Best for:

- easiest group consensus
- food, shopping, and nicer evenings
- flexible split days with minimal coordination

Main weakness:

- may feel too city-heavy for Phil and Lillian

### Tokyo + Okinawa

File: `02-japan-tokyo-okinawa.md`

Best for:

- warm-weather and beach fantasy
- resort energy
- diving and snorkeling if conditions cooperate

Main weakness:

- most weather-sensitive option
- most flight-heavy option

### Tokyo + Hokkaido

File: `03-japan-tokyo-hokkaido.md`

Best for:

- strongest June weather position
- big nature, photography, and active travel
- keeping Tokyo while still making the trip feel different

Main weakness:

- weaker warm-weather appeal
- likely wants one flight and possibly a car

### Tokyo + Hokuriku + Alps

File: `04-japan-tokyo-hokuriku-alps.md`

Best for:

- strongest regional-town and mountain route
- hiking, photography, and old-town atmosphere
- a more unusual repeat-Japan trip

Main weakness:

- more coordination and transfer friction
- less day-to-day flexibility

### Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku

File: `05-japan-tokyo-setouchi-shikoku.md`

Best for:

- lower city share without going full expedition mode
- cycling, ferries, islands, art, and seaside towns
- active novelty without needing a rugged trip

Main weakness:

- weaker shopping and nightlife than Kansai
- still June weather, just in a less fragile format

2. Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku
3. Tokyo + Hokkaido
4. Tokyo + Hokuriku + Alps

Why this ranking:

- reduce flying from San Francisco
- preserve Tokyo

## Recommended shortlist

If you want the simplest decision set, focus on these two:

- `Tokyo + Izu + Kansai`
- `Tokyo + Setouchi + Shikoku`

Add `Tokyo + Hokkaido` only if June weather confidence matters more than warm/coastal energy.
